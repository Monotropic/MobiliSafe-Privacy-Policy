MobiliSafe™ App Privacy Policy

InterMotive, Inc. designed the MobiliSafe™ app as a professional tool for vehicle safety monitoring. This service is provided by InterMotive, Inc. at no cost to the end user and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies on the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when using our service.

CRITICAL DISCLOSURE: Background Location Access

MobiliSafe collects location data to enable live telematics and wheelchair position monitoring even when the app is closed or not in use. This background access is essential for dispatchers to monitor the real-time status of wheelchair positions on buses and vans to ensure occupant safety and provide timely emergency response. This data is only used for operational safety and is never shared for advertising purposes.

Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, while using our service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

  • Precise Location Data: Used for telematics and safety monitoring (including background access).
  • Device Information: To ensure compatibility with wheelchair monitoring hardware.
  • Phone Number/State: For account identification and coordination with dispatch.

The information that we request will be retained by InterMotive, Inc. and used as described in this Privacy Policy.
