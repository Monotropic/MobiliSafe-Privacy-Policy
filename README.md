## **Project Overview**

MobiliSafe™ is a specialized wheelchair position monitoring technology designed for transit buses and vans. The app enables real-time safety monitoring to ensure the security of occupants during transit.

## **Compliance & Privacy**

This documentation has been specifically updated to meet the **Google Play Prominent Disclosure and Consent Requirement**.

### **Key Disclosure: Background Location Access**

To fulfill its primary safety function, MobiliSafe™ requires access to location data even when the app is closed or not in use. This data is used exclusively to:

* Provide live telematics to dispatchers.  
* Monitor wheelchair position status in real-time.  
* Ensure occupant safety and facilitate emergency response.

## **Hosting Instructions**

1. Upload the index.html file to this repository.  
2. Navigate to **Settings \> Pages**.  
3. Under **Build and deployment**, set the source to "Deploy from a branch".  
4. Select the main branch and /root folder, then click **Save**.  
5. Once the site is live, provide the URL to the Google Play Console under **App Content \> Privacy Policy**.
